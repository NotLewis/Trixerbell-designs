Luxury Gift Experience

Challenge

Develop a unique style that can be used throughout LGE's marketing and social media platforms to help draw in new customers.

Solution

Close collaboration with LGE allowed us to identify what the client needed, and how they needed it to be delivered.

Results

An engaging new house style including several marketing products and a new website design.
